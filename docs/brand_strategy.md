# Convergence Workspace: Brand & Experience Manifest

## Brand Identity
Convergence Workspace exists at the intersection of surgical technical precision and deep mindful focus.

## UX Goals
- **Intuitive Navigation**: Gesture-based HUD controls.
- **Calming Interactions**: Soft haptic feedback and "breathing" UI.
- **Uncluttered Layouts**: Surgical glass layering for high-density data.
- **Smooth Transitions**: 120Hz-ready cinematic motion.
